Introduction

Welcome on {{ state.appName }}. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and password (encrypted). This information is necessary to provide you with access to our social network features and enable recipe synchronization. You can also add a profile picture after account creation but it won't be visible to other users on this version.

Recipe & Social Content

We store recipes you create and share, including titles, ingredients, instructions, images, comments, likes, and any metadata you provide. This data is stored locally on your device and synced to our servers to enable social sharing and interaction with other users. Your public recipes are visible to other {{ state.appName }} users.

Camera & Images

Our app requests camera permission to allow you to take photos of your recipes. Images are stored locally and synced to our servers when you choose to share them. We do not access your camera without your explicit permission.

Social Interactions

We collect information about your interactions on the platform, including recipes you like, comments you post, users you follow, and content you share. This helps us personalize your experience and improve our social features.

Technical Information

We do not collect any technical information.

Data Storage & Synchronization

Local Storage: Your recipes and social data are stored locally on your device in a secure database. You can use the app offline to view and create recipes.

Cloud Synchronization: When you enable sync, your recipes and social interactions are transmitted to our servers. You control when synchronization occurs and can choose to sync updates from the server to your device or upload your local changes. Public recipes and social content are stored on our servers to enable sharing with other users.

Data Security: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard protocols (HTTPS/TLS). Your password is encrypted and never stored in plain text.

Content Sharing & Visibility

Public Content: When you share recipes publicly on {{ state.appName }}, they become visible to all users of the platform. Other users can view, like, comment on, and share your public recipes.

Private Content: Recipes marked as private are only visible to you and are not shared with other users unless you explicitly change their visibility settings.

Profile Information: Your profile information (username, profile picture, bio) is visible to other users to facilitate social interactions. You can control what information appears on your profile through privacy settings.

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With other {{ state.appName }} users when you share public content
  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations or valid legal requests
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users and the community
  • With service providers who assist in operating our app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. However, if you are under 13 years old (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction), we require parental consent before using our services. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

Your Rights & Choices

Access Your Data

You can access and view all your recipe data and social content at any time through the app.

Correction

You can edit or correct your account information, profile, and recipe data directly within the app.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data from the app settings. This action is irreversible and will permanently remove all your recipes, social interactions, and personal information.

Privacy Controls

You can control the visibility of your recipes (public/private).

Sync Control

You can avoid synchronization to keep your data local-only and control what content is shared publicly.

Notifications

There is no notification feature in the app at the moment.

Data Retention

All you data including recipes and social interactions are retained as long as your account is active. If you choose to delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed from our servers and cannot be recovered.

  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Secure server infrastructure with industry-standard protections
  • Content moderation to protect community safety
